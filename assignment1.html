This is a waffle chart showing percentage of each zoning district taking place in Manhattanville. The 20 by 20 grid block represents the total zoning area of Manhattanville, and each unit represents one street block. The sum of all the type of zoning district is the whole 20 by 20 block.

Waffle chart is different from a traditional pie chart that relies on angles and arc lengths. Waffle chart offers an opportunity to visualize data in an elegant and neat way, and audience could view each piece of information one at a time.
